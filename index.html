Frequently Asked Questions

What is Shrunk?

Shrunk tracks shrinkflation — when companies reduce product sizes while keeping prices the same or higher. Scan barcodes, compare sizes over time, and see which products are shrinking the most.

How do I scan a product?

Tap the Scan tab, point your camera at any barcode, and Shrunk will look it up instantly. We check our database of 47,000+ US products first, then search external sources if needed.

What does Shrunk Premium include?

Premium ($4.99/month) unlocks full shrinkflation history, the ability to submit reports, community features, direct messaging, and removes all ads.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Profile, then tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage or cancel.

A product is missing or has wrong info

Our database grows every day through user reports. If you find incorrect data, you can submit a report with the correct size and price, or email us and we'll fix it.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at shrunkapp@gmail.com with your request and we'll process it within 48 hours.
